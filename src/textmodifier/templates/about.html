TEXT MODIFIER
This is a TextUtil Web Application. Here you can Modify Your Texts

Remove Punctuations : Remove Punctuation will helps you to remove all Punctuations from your text.
Word Count : Counts Each word,Single character,Sentences,Prepositions.
make PDF : Clean Your Text First.Then use it.

Text To Speech : Converts your text to speech.

Text to QrCode = Qr code Refers to Quick Response code. Give a limited text because huge text can't response quick.
